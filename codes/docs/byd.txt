BYD (Build Your Dreams) is a Chinese multinational company founded in 1995 and headquartered in Shenzhen, China.

BYD originally started as a rechargeable battery manufacturer and later expanded into electric vehicles, buses, trucks, and renewable energy technologies.

The company is led by founder and CEO Wang Chuanfu. BYD has become one of the largest electric vehicle manufacturers in the world.

BYD reported approximately $83 billion in revenue in 2023. The company sells electric passenger vehicles, electric buses, and hybrid vehicles.

BYD dominates the electric vehicle market in China and has expanded rapidly into international markets including Europe, Latin America, and Southeast Asia.

The company produces its own batteries using Blade Battery technology which focuses on safety, long lifespan, and improved energy density.

BYD has become one of Tesla's biggest competitors in the global electric vehicle industry.
