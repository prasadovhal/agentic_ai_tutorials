Battery technology is one of the most critical components of electric vehicles.

Most electric vehicles today use lithium-ion batteries due to their high energy density and long cycle life.

Battery cost represents a significant portion of the total cost of an electric vehicle. Reducing battery cost is essential for making EVs more affordable.

Several companies are investing in next generation battery technologies such as solid-state batteries, lithium iron phosphate batteries, and advanced lithium-ion chemistries.

Tesla works with companies such as Panasonic and CATL for battery manufacturing, while also developing its own battery technology.

BYD produces its own Blade Battery, which is based on lithium iron phosphate chemistry and designed to improve safety and durability.

Future innovations in battery technology are expected to increase driving range, reduce charging time, and lower overall costs of electric vehicles.
