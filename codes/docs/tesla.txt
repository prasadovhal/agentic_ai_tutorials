Tesla is an American electric vehicle and clean energy company founded in 2003. The company was founded by Martin Eberhard and Marc Tarpenning. Elon Musk joined Tesla in 2004 as chairman and later became CEO.

Tesla is headquartered in Austin, Texas, United States. The company designs and manufactures electric vehicles, battery energy storage systems, solar panels, and solar roof products.

Tesla reported approximately $96 billion in revenue in 2023. The company produces several electric vehicle models including Model S, Model 3, Model X, Model Y, and Cybertruck.

Tesla dominates the electric vehicle market in the United States. Model Y became one of the best selling electric vehicles globally.

Tesla also develops autonomous driving technologies under its Full Self Driving (FSD) program. The company operates large gigafactories in Texas, Shanghai, Berlin, and Nevada.

Tesla is considered one of the most influential companies driving the global transition toward sustainable transportation.
